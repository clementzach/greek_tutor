Usage Guide

This guide shows examples of what you can ask the tutor to do. Write naturally — the agent understands plain English. When helpful, it will use tools to fetch data or perform actions.

Concepts & Explanations

  • Explain a concept: “Explain the aorist tense with beginner-friendly examples.”
  • Ask follow‑ups: “How is the genitive used with πίστις?”

Greek NT & KJV Passages

  • Get GNT verses: “Show John 1:1–3 in Greek.”
  • Get KJV verses: “Give me the KJV for John 1:1–3.”
  • Align and explain: “Explain how John 1:1 maps from Greek to KJV, word by word.”

Vocabulary

  • Generate vocab — global: “Add the top 20 most common NT words to my vocab.”
  • Generate vocab — by book: “Add 30 common words from Galatians.”
  • Generate vocab — by chapter: “Add 15 common words from John 3.”
  • Review suggestions: “Give me relevant vocabulary to review.”
  • Record mastery explicitly after a session: “Mark that I’ve mastered the concept ‘Present Active Indicative.’”

Record Interests

  • Topics: “I’m interested in parsing participles.”
  • Books/Chapters: “I want to study Galatians” or “Focus on John 3.”
  • Passages: “I’m interested in John 3:16–18.”

These are stored so the tutor can tailor examples and vocab. You can also record them on the Dashboard.

Quiz Mode

  • Start a vocab quiz: “Quiz me on vocabulary in Galatians.”
  • Answer in English; the tutor grades answers as correct/partial/incorrect and tracks progress.
  • End anytime: “End quiz.”

Account & Level

  • Set level (explicit only): “Set my level to Beginner” or “I am B1.”
  • Note: The tutor will not change your level based on quiz answers.

Troubleshooting

  • If passages don’t load, ensure the datasets are downloaded (Dashboard → run the download script or see README).
  • If vocab generation inserts 0 words, try a different scope, increase count, or ensure the full GNT is installed.
